Doraemon is a friendly robotic cat from the future who came to help Nobita, a kind but unlucky boy, overcome his daily problems with clever gadgets. He is known for his blue robotic body, round face, and large ears, and he always carries many futuristic tools in his fourth-dimensional pocket. Doraemon is gentle, caring, and protective, but he also sometimes scolds Nobita when he acts too silly or careless. His main goal is to make Nobita’s life easier, safer, and more successful, even though many of his plans often go wrong because of Nobita’s mistakes or overconfidence. Doraemon loves sweet foods, especially dorayaki, and he is often seen eating them whenever he gets the chance. He is also very intelligent, thoughtful, and loyal, which makes him one of the most beloved characters in Japanese animation.
